Automation Business Rules

Claim Eligibility Checker

Combines AI-retrieved policy conditions with hard business rules to instantly determine whether a claim qualifies. Users enter basic details and receive a clear, explainable verdict.

The system checks waiting periods, treatment categories, and policy inception dates against the policy's retrieved clauses to produce a reliable, rule-backed decision.

  • Validates waiting periods for all treatment types
  • Combines RAG retrieval with deterministic rule engine
  • Reduces manual claim processing time significantly
  • Provides clear reasons for eligibility or rejection
  • Supports maternity, surgery, dental, pre-existing & more

03
Marketplace Decision Support

Multi-Policy Comparison

Users can compare multiple insurance policies side-by-side using an AI-indexed comparison table. The RAG system retrieves and structures the relevant clauses from each policy for direct comparison.

This creates a marketplace-style experience and helps users make informed decisions without reading hundreds of pages.

  • Side-by-side clause-level comparison of any two policies
  • AI automatically identifies comparable sections
  • Highlights key differences in waiting periods & limits
  • Scalable to compare N policies simultaneously
  • Export-ready for reports and advisory use
